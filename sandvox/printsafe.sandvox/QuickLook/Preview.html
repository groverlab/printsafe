The PrintSafe Project


Currently under development, The PrintSafe Project will inform 3D printer users about the potential hazards of their printers and educate users about safe printer use.

The leader of The PrintSafe Project is Prof. William Grover of the Bioengineering Department at the University of California, Riverside.
