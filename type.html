What type of material does your 3D printer use?


  • Filament

    My printer uses a spool of plastic filament. It passes the filament through a hot nozzle that melts it into a 3D-printed part, kinda like a hot glue gun.


  • Liquid

    My printer uses bottles of liquid resin. It uses light to turn the liquid resin into a solid 3D-printed part.


  • Powder

    My printer uses powder. It uses a liquid binder to glue the powder into a solid 3D-printed part.


  • Something else...

    My printer doesn't use plastic filament or liquid resin...
